About Us

Our Journey Since 2010

AJ Fruits and Vegetables Located at Nakasero Market, Stall 281, AJ Fruits and Vegetables Supply is your go-to source for premium produce. Founded in 2010 as a family-owned market stall, we have grown into a trusted provider of fresh, farm-to-door fruits and vegetables. We offer a wide variety of local and imported fruits, including apples, oranges, bananas, grapes, kiwis, and more, as well as an extensive selection of vegetables. With a commitment to quality and convenience, we also provide reliable delivery services countrywide.

Our founder, Kim Isma, believed in creating a direct bridge between local farmers and consumers, ensuring that families have access to the freshest, highest-quality produce while supporting sustainable farming practices.
